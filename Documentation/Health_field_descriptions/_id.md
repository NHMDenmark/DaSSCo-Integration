## "_id"

**Type of field:**  
String  

**Part of which document:**

**Description:**  
Unique identifier for the entry. Autogenerated by mongo db.   

**Value:**  
Random string.

**Why do we have this field:**  
Needed as the database unique identifier.  

**Populated by whom and when:**

Never
